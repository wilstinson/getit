William Stinson

Front-End Web-Developer & Audio Engineer

I create films, develop websites, produce music, day trade, install electrical work and teach Brazilian Jiu Jitsu

Since an early age i've always excelled in one main area, creativity.
From picking up a guitar, to trying (and failing) to "improve" my electronic items, to eventually finding my creative path across multiple platforms.
Creativity was always at the heart of everything i ever did and will be at the heart of everything i go on to do.

Whilst electrical has always been my "back-up job". Audio production was my first creative endeavour. I began with local talent, offering my services for free just so i could learn, this quickly turned into paid gigs and only snowballed from there.
Design came second, largely as a means to offer artists a "full package", everything from recording music to designing cover arts and web images and then into video productions.
Basic Web Design, HTML and CSS was implemented and engrained early (thanks Myspace!) however was left behind as my focus on music grew.

I eventually found myself uninspired amongst my current creative passions, longing to be the absolute learner again. This is when film making and web development really took hold.
I was instantly hooked, with creativity at the forefront of an endless pursuit of technical knowledge.
In the space of only 12 months, i developed from a relatively clueless consumer of web sites and videos, into the creator of various projects. Ranging from free passion projects right up to highly paid professional works.

It was becoming evidently clear that my previously forged path, of being an electrician, was no longer able to sustain my pursuit for creativity..
